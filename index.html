Community Martial Arts Foundation

The Community Martial Arts Foundation is a 501(c3) not-for-profit organization who's mission is to foster mentoring and community service from the martial arts community, to the public at large, through youth development and community education.

Scholarship

CMAF Provides scholarships for interested "at risk" or "at need" students to participate in Qualified Programs that have been shown to teach a traditional system of martial arts that promote character development, life skills, respect for all people, and focus on self actualization.

Community Education

We help provide education about safety, fitness, self-control, culture and history. CMAF also helps raise money for general needs in the community through martial arts events (e.g. Martial Arts activity fundraisers for foodbanks or cancer patients.).

Youth Development

We are always in need sponsors to help us provide uniforms and equipement, testing fees, holiday gifts, and mentoring expenses. Your support can also help make student travel affordable to tournaments and cultural events with uplifting and educational components.
